# 2026-04-05 Roadmap Split

## Context

The repository now contains two distinct work streams:

- the active DaXin delivery baseline
- the Chimei-inspired AI-assisted home nursing discovery direction

This note records the decision to separate them in planning and prioritization.

## Participants

- Repository owner
- Codex

## Key Decisions

- The project roadmap is split into `Delivery Track` and `Discovery Track`
- `Delivery Track` remains the active priority for execution and customer-facing progress
- `Discovery Track` remains research and future planning unless explicitly promoted into scope
- The Chimei PDF is treated as structured product input, not proof of current deliverability

## Rationale

- Mixing current customer commitments with future AI exploration would create avoidable scope confusion
- The DaXin materials already define a practical near-term implementation direction
- The Chimei material provides strong workflow insight, but still requires governance, safety, and validation work

## Open Questions

- When should AI-assisted documentation move from discovery into active product scope?
- Should the repository remain primarily project-delivery oriented, or evolve into a product repository?
- What additional governance documents are needed if voice and generated note workflows are explored further?

## Action Items

- Keep `docs/roadmap.md` as the current planning reference
- Keep `docs/requirements.md`, `docs/spec.md`, and `docs/decisions.md` aligned with the split
- Translate the DaXin delivery track into implementation-ready work items
- Keep Chimei-inspired ideas in discovery until a formal phase decision is made

## Follow-up Date
